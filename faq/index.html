Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BloxGen.
What is Bloxgen?

BloxGen is an automated service that generates Roblox accounts quickly and efficiently. And it has more separate services such as scrapers, snipers and group finder

How can I be sure that the accounts are legitimate?

All of our accounts are legitimately generated and comply with Roblox 's terms and conditions. In addition, we have implemented security measures to ensure the protection of your personal information.

How do we get old accounts?

We obtain the accounts using the bloxgen ia scrapers, which uses the roblox APIs to obtain the accounts and passwords.

How can I generate an account?

Go to our discord server and go to the channel called generator and type /generate and you will get a slash command and you must give it and choose the account option you want.

Are the accounts restricted?

No, our accounts are not logged in on any device and no one has them.

The accounts work 100%?

Of course yes, our accounts are checked when the generate command is executed and a working account is obtained.

What happens if I find an error?

If you find a bug contact us as soon as you can in discord open ticket support and we will fix it.

Can i pay with robux?

Yes, in these cases you have to open a discord support ticket and follow the staff 's instructions to pay with Robux.
